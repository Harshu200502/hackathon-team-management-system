## Stakeholder Register

The stakeholder register provides a formal record of all stakeholders associated with the **Hackathon Team Management System**, including their roles, interests, and level of influence on the project.

---

| Stakeholder ID | Stakeholder Name | Role / Category | Interest in Project | Influence Level |
|---------------|-----------------|-----------------|---------------------|-----------------|
| STK-01 | Hackathon Participants | Primary | Easy team joining, clear communication, and role clarity | Medium |
| STK-02 | Team Leaders | Primary | Efficient team creation, member management, and coordination | High |
| STK-03 | Hackathon Organizers | Secondary | Organized team formation and smooth event management | High |
| STK-04 | Project Development Team | Primary | Successful project implementation and learning outcomes | High |
| STK-05 | Judges / Evaluators | Secondary | Functional completeness and proper documentation | Medium |
| STK-06 | Academic Institution | Secondary | Achievement of SEAD objectives and academic evaluation | Medium |
| STK-07 | Future Users | Secondary | Scalable and reusable system for future hackathons | Low |
