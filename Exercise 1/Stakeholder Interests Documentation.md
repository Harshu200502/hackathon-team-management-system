## Stakeholder Interests Documentation

This section documents the interests and level of influence of stakeholders involved in the **Hackathon Team Management System**. Understanding stakeholder interests helps ensure that system requirements align with user needs and project goals.

---

### Stakeholder Interests and Influence

| Stakeholder | Interests | Level of Influence |
|------------|-----------|-------------------|
| Hackathon Participants | Easy team joining, clear role assignment, smooth collaboration, and minimal manual effort. | Medium |
| Team Leaders | Efficient team creation, member management, role allocation, and progress tracking. | High |
| Hackathon Organizers | Organized team formation, fair participation, and overall event coordination. | High |
| Project Development Team | Successful implementation, learning outcomes, and timely project completion. | High |
| Judges / Evaluators | Well-documented system, functional completeness, and effective collaboration features. | Medium |
| Academic Institution | Achievement of SEAD learning objectives and quality project deliverables. | Medium |
| Future Users | Scalable, reusable, and user-friendly system for future events. | Low |
