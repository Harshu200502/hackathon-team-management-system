The following features and functionalities are **excluded** from the scope of the **Hackathon Team Management System** for the current project phase. These items are not planned for implementation due to time, resource, or scope limitations.

---

### Out-of-Scope Features

- Payment processing or financial transactions  
- Integration with external platforms (email services, social media, third-party APIs)  
- Real-time chat or video conferencing features  
- Advanced analytics and reporting dashboards  
- Mobile application (Android / iOS) development  
- AI-based team recommendations or matching  
- Multi-event or multi-hackathon management  
- Admin-level moderation and control panels  
- Data backup, recovery, and high-availability mechanisms  

---

>  **Note:**  
> Any feature not explicitly listed under the *In-Scope* section is considered out of scope for the current SEAD Hackathon project and may be considered for future enhancements.
