## Stakeholder Register

The stakeholder register identifies key stakeholders involved in the **Hackathon Team Management System** project along with their roles, interests, and level of influence.

| Stakeholder Name / Group | Role | Interest in Project | Influence Level |
|--------------------------|------|---------------------|-----------------|
| Students (Participants) | Primary Users | Easy team formation, clear roles, smooth collaboration | High |
| Team Leaders | Team Coordinators | Efficient team management and communication | High |
| Hackathon Organizers / Admin | System Administrators | Monitoring teams, smooth event execution | High |
| Project Development Team | Developers | Successful implementation and delivery | Medium |
| Faculty / Evaluators | Reviewers | Project quality and documentation | Medium |
| Institution / College | Indirect Stakeholder | Student skill development and project outcomes | Low |

### Notes
- This register will be reviewed and updated if new stakeholders are identified.
- Influence levels are classified as **High / Medium / Low** based on decision-making power.

### Submission Confirmation
This stakeholder register is submitted as part of the project documentation for the **Hackathon Team Management System**.
