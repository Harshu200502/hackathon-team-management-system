## Stakeholder Analysis

Stakeholders are individuals or groups who have an interest in or are affected by the **Hackathon Team Management System**. They are categorized as **primary** and **secondary** stakeholders based on their involvement with the system.

---

### Primary Stakeholders

| Stakeholder | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| Hackathon Participants | Use the system to join teams, view team details, and collaborate with members. |
| Team Leaders | Create teams, invite members, assign roles, and manage team activities. |
| Project Development Team | Design, develop, test, and maintain the system throughout the project lifecycle. |

---

### Secondary Stakeholders

| Stakeholder | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| Hackathon Organizers | Oversee team formation and ensure smooth coordination during events. |
| Judges / Evaluators | Evaluate system functionality, usability, and team collaboration effectiveness. |
| Academic Institution | Uses the project for academic assessment and learning outcomes (SEAD). |
| Future Users | Potential users who may adopt the system for future hackathons or events. |
