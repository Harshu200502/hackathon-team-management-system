## Project Charter

### Project Title
Hackathon Team Management System

### Project Overview
The Hackathon Team Management System is a web-based application designed to simplify team formation, role assignment, and coordination during hackathons and technical events. The system provides a centralized platform to reduce manual effort and improve collaboration among participants.

### Project Objectives
- Enable easy creation and management of hackathon teams  
- Improve clarity of team roles and responsibilities  
- Enhance communication and coordination among team members  
- Reduce administrative overhead during hackathon events  

### Scope Summary
**In-Scope:**
- User registration and login  
- Team creation and member management  
- Role assignment within teams  
- Invitation and join team functionality  

**Out-of-Scope:**
- Online coding environment  
- Payment processing  
- Third-party integrations  

### Key Stakeholders
- Hackathon Participants (Students)  
- Team Leaders  
- Hackathon Organizers/Admin  
- Project Development Team  

### Project Deliverables
- Software Requirements Specification (SRS)  
- UML Diagrams (Use Case, Sequence, Class)  
- Functional and Non-Functional Requirements  
- Working prototype (if applicable)  
- Project documentation  

### Assumptions and Constraints
- Users have basic internet access  
- Project is developed within the hackathon timeline  
- Limited resources and fixed deadlines
